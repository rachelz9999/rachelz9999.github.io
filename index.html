Book Reviews

  • Calamity

  • Calamity by Brandon Sanderson is the third installment of a fantastic trilogy about Epics, a post-apocalyptic race of humans with superpowers, and an ordinary yet driven young man named David. Like the biblical context of his name, David is set out to slay Goliath: to complete his revenge by ridding Earth of power-corrupted Epics. This is an unique series that explores human nature. Are we born inherently good, evil, or neither? It depicts the corruption that power ultimately brings, but also explains the potential of humans when they overcome their fears to change the world.

Film

  • Character Analysis: Zuko

  • Zuko is my favorite character in the Avatar: The Last Airbender series, and perhaps ever. He is so complex, so realistic I have no problem fully empathizing and understanding him. His status as an antihero is what appeals to me so much. I love how he has flaws, and struggles to do the right thing. He represents all of us and the human condition to struggle forward to make the right choice and create meaning for ourselves. That is what makes his character so powerful.
